About AshX

Who Is AshX?

I'm Rathod Ashvin from Gujarat — a self-taught Instagram creator who builds faceless pages from scratch. No team. No budget. Just consistency and the hunger to learn.

Started in 2022 with zero knowledge. My first page @ashadits_ grew to 3.4K followers before it got banned. That failure taught me the most. Instead of quitting, I used the earnings to buy my first products and kept going.

I later tried a football page (Goalxnews) but it didn't work. So I rebranded it to CricLovex. In 2025, I launched my second page — The Mind Word.

Today I run both pages. My cricket page CricLovex alone crossed 23M+ views in 30 days (March-April 2026)

The Struggle
Started with nothing — no mentor, no money, no guidance. Got pages banned and made every mistake possible. That's why I now understand what actually works.
The Breakthrough
After my first page got banned, I applied everything I learned and built two stronger pages in different niches.
The Goal
Grow both pages to 10K+ followers, create a digital guide, and prove that anyone can build a profitable faceless Instagram page — no face required.
