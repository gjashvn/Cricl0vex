About AshX

Who Is AshX?

I'm Rathod Ashvin — a self-taught Instagram creator who builds faceless pages from scratch. No team. No budget. Just consistency and strategy.

Started in 2022 with zero knowledge — got my first page banned because I didn't understand the rules. Most people quit there. I didn't.

Today I run CricLovex and The Mind Word — two pages in completely different niches, both generating real views and real income. My cricket page alone crossed 23M+ views in 90 days with one reel hitting 10.1M organically.

The Struggle
Started with nothing — no mentor, no shortcut. Got accounts banned. Wasted months. Made every mistake. That's exactly why I understand what actually works.
The Breakthrough
@ashadits_ crossed 3K followers and 10M+ views before getting suspended in July 2025. Instead of stopping — I launched two stronger pages with everything I'd learned.
The Goal
Scale both pages to 10K+ followers, launch a digital guide, and prove that anyone can build a page that pays — no face required.
